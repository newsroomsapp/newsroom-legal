NEWSROOM COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

Newsroom is a platform for sharing and discovering news through user-generated video content. These Community Guidelines exist to ensure Newsroom remains a safe, respectful, and trustworthy environment for all users.

By using Newsroom, you agree to follow these guidelines. Violations may result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent termination.

1. CONTENT STANDARDS

1.1 BE TRUTHFUL
- Share news you believe to be accurate and factual
- Clearly distinguish between facts and opinions
- Do not intentionally spread misinformation or disinformation
- Do not create fake or manipulated content designed to deceive

1.2 BE ORIGINAL
- Only post content you have created or have permission to share
- Do not infringe on copyrights, trademarks, or other intellectual property rights
- Give credit where credit is due

1.3 BE APPROPRIATE
- Content must be suitable for users ages 17 and older
- Do not post sexually explicit or pornographic content
- Do not post excessively graphic violence or gore
- Use content warnings when sharing sensitive material

2. PROHIBITED CONTENT

The following content is strictly prohibited on Newsroom:

2.1 VIOLENCE AND THREATS
- Threats of violence against individuals or groups
- Content that promotes, glorifies, or incites violence
- Graphic violence intended to shock or disturb
- Content promoting terrorism or violent extremism

2.2 HATE SPEECH AND DISCRIMINATION
- Content that attacks individuals or groups based on:
  - Race or ethnicity
  - National origin
  - Religion
  - Gender or gender identity
  - Sexual orientation
  - Disability
  - Age
  - Veteran status
  - Any other protected characteristic
- Slurs, stereotypes, or dehumanizing language
- Support for hate groups or ideologies

2.3 HARASSMENT AND BULLYING
- Personal attacks or insults directed at individuals
- Targeted harassment campaigns
- Doxxing (sharing private information without consent)
- Encouraging others to harass someone
- Revenge content or content intended to shame someone

2.4 CHILD SAFETY
- Any content that sexualizes, exploits, or endangers minors
- Content depicting child abuse or neglect
- Interactions intended to groom or exploit minors
- This is a zero-tolerance policy and will be reported to authorities

2.5 SELF-HARM AND SUICIDE
- Content that promotes or glorifies self-harm or suicide
- Content providing instructions for self-harm
- If you or someone you know is struggling, please contact:
  - National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988
  - Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741

2.6 ILLEGAL ACTIVITIES
- Content promoting or facilitating illegal activities
- Drug trafficking or sales
- Weapons sales or illegal weapon modifications
- Fraud, scams, or financial crimes
- Human trafficking

2.7 REGULATED GOODS AND SERVICES
- Sale or promotion of illegal drugs
- Sale of firearms or ammunition (unless properly licensed)
- Sale of alcohol or tobacco to minors
- Gambling or betting services (unless properly licensed)

2.8 PRIVACY VIOLATIONS
- Sharing others' personal information without consent
- Sharing private communications without consent
- Recording people in private settings without consent
- Sharing intimate images without consent

2.9 SPAM AND MANIPULATION
- Repetitive, unwanted, or unsolicited content
- Fake engagement or artificial amplification
- Coordinated inauthentic behavior
- Misleading links or deceptive practices

2.10 IMPERSONATION
- Pretending to be another person or organization
- Creating accounts to mislead others about your identity
- Parody or fan accounts must be clearly labeled

3. BEHAVIORAL STANDARDS

3.1 RESPECT OTHERS
- Treat all users with dignity and respect
- Engage in good-faith discussions
- Accept that others may have different viewpoints
- Disagree respectfully without personal attacks

3.2 PROTECT THE COMMUNITY
- Report content that violates these guidelines
- Block users who harass or disturb you
- Do not engage with trolls or bad actors
- Help new users understand community norms

3.3 USE FEATURES RESPONSIBLY
- Use the voting system to highlight important news, not to harass
- Use the follow feature genuinely, not to stalk
- Use the reporting feature for genuine violations, not to silence opinions you disagree with

4. ENFORCEMENT

4.1 HOW WE ENFORCE
We use a combination of:
- User reports
- Automated systems
- Human review

4.2 ACTIONS WE MAY TAKE
Depending on the severity of the violation:
- Warning
- Content removal
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent account termination
- Reporting to law enforcement (for illegal activity)

4.3 FACTORS WE CONSIDER
- Severity of the violation
- History of violations
- Intent behind the content
- Potential for harm
- Context of the content

4.4 APPEALS
If you believe we made an error, you can appeal by contacting us at canzel216@gmail.com. Please include:
- Your username
- Description of the content or action
- Why you believe our decision was incorrect

5. SPECIAL CONSIDERATIONS

5.1 NEWS CONTENT
Because Newsroom focuses on news:
- Breaking news may contain graphic content - use discretion
- Distinguish between reporting on violence and glorifying it
- Sensitive topics require thoughtful presentation
- Consider the public interest when sharing news

5.2 PUBLIC FIGURES
- Public figures may be subject to more commentary and criticism
- This does not permit harassment, threats, or false statements
- Private information about public figures is still protected

5.3 SATIRE AND COMMENTARY
- Satire, parody, and social commentary are permitted
- Must be clearly identifiable as such
- Must not cross into harassment or defamation

6. YOUR SAFETY

6.1 PROTECT YOURSELF
- Do not share personal information publicly
- Use a strong, unique password
- Be cautious about meeting people from the internet
- Report suspicious behavior

6.2 IF YOU FEEL UNSAFE
- Block the user immediately
- Report the content or user
- Contact local law enforcement if there is an immediate threat
- Reach out to us at canzel216@gmail.com

6.3 MENTAL HEALTH RESOURCES
If you are struggling, help is available:
- National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988
- Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
- SAMHSA National Helpline: 1-800-662-4357

7. CHANGES TO GUIDELINES

We may update these Community Guidelines from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App. Continued use of Newsroom after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated guidelines.
